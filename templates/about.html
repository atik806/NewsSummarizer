About NewsSummarizer

Our Mission

NewsSummarizer is dedicated to providing quick and accurate summaries of news articles from leading Bangladeshi newspapers. Our goal is to help readers stay informed without spending hours reading through multiple sources.

How It Works

Using advanced web scraping and natural language processing technologies, we:

  • Fetch news articles from multiple sources in real-time
  • Extract and analyze the content using AI algorithms
  • Generate concise summaries while preserving key information
  • Present the news in an easy-to-read format

News Sources

We currently aggregate news from:

  • BDNews24
  • Prothom Alo
  • Jugantor
  • Ittefaq

Technology Stack

Our platform is built using:

  • Python Flask for the backend
  • BeautifulSoup and Newspaper3k for web scraping
  • Sumy for text summarization
  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the frontend

Privacy & Ethics

We are committed to:

  • Respecting copyright and fair use policies
  • Providing proper attribution to original sources
  • Maintaining user privacy and data security
  • Ensuring unbiased and accurate news representation
